Turn your device into a personal beat pad with OctoTaps! Customize each of the eight sound pads by swapping sounds, adding from your library, or recording your own. Whether you're a musician, DJ, or just love playing with sounds, OctoTaps makes beat-making easy, fun, and uniquely yours.

🌟 Unique Features

  • 🎛️ Custom Sound Pads: Swap and personalize each pad’s sound.
  • 📁 Library Access: Import any audio file directly from your device.
  • 🎙️ Record & Add: Capture and use your own recordings.
  • 🔊 High-Quality Audio Playback: Every tap sounds great!

🛠️ Tools & Tech

  • Unity (Built-in Pipeline)
  • Photoshop & Audacity for polish
  • Fl Studio
