charming platformer built in Blender Game Engine, Zumper: The Adventure follows the story of a brave cube hero on a mission to rescue his kidnapped villagers from the clutches of the evil devil Juba.
🌍 Designed with a simple yet stylized world, Zumper’s journey takes players through levels filled with traps, challenges, and light combat , all created using Blender’s native game engine. This project was my creative experiment combining storytelling with level design inside Blender.

🌟 Unique Features

  • 🎮 Platformer Gameplay: Classic jump-and-run mechanics with simple level progression.
  • 🧠 Puzzle Elements: Navigate through traps and timed platforms to reach the end.
  • 🦾 Enemy Encounters: Basic AI that challenges the player’s movement and timing.
  • 📜 Light Narrative: A short story-driven setup to give purpose and motivation.
  • 🌍 Stylized Visuals: Minimalist cube-based characters and environments.
  • 🎯 Casual Difficulty: Designed to be accessible and easy to complete in one sitting.

🛠️ Tools & Tech

  • 🧱 Blender Game Engine: Core engine used for gameplay and logic.
  • 🎨 Blender: Modeling, animation, and level design.
  • 🖼️ Photoshop: UI elements and texture design.
  • 💻 Windows Build: Game exported and tested for PC.
